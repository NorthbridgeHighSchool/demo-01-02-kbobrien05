The Books I Read Over the Summer

I love to read. So although I wasn't thrilled with the idea of spending months staring at my chromebook for virtual school, I was happy to learn that I had extra time to read in the summer. I was able to read 19 books this summer!

My two favorite books from the whole summer were "One of Us is Lying" by Karen M. McManus and "All the Bright Places" by Jennifer Niven. I typically mainly read sci-fi, but this summer I started reading a lot more contemporaries and even a few mystery and fantasy novels."One of Us is Lying" is a murder mystery while "All the Bright Places" is a contemporary that discusses mental illnesses like bipolar disorder.
